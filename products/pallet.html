Hexsa Pallets

Hexsa Pallets are made with 30 mm Hexsa Hi-Flex Honecomb board and reinforced with paper corner angles, our pallets offer exceptional load bearing capacity and flexural stiffness.

Four way and two way pallets can be custom designed for container optimization.

Legs are anchored with high grade plastic rivets.

Pallets are capable of handling loads upto 2 tons.

Coated with moisture resistant bio-degradable polymer.

Ideal for shipping export goods as they are light weight, eco-friendly, Exempt from ISPM 15 norms
